About me

Profile

Final-year Computer Science Engineering student at Sri Eshwar College of Engineering, Tamil Nadu — and the sole computational biology researcher at my institution. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning and living systems: I develop federated learning frameworks for high-dimensional biological data where samples are scarce and features are extreme.

My primary research output is REEF (Robust Expert Encoder Federation), a vertical federated learning framework for coral multi-omics stress response analysis, published on arXiv (2601.00050). I also have an ongoing paper proposing a novel federated inference architecture for protein structure prediction using AlphaFold2, where institutional silos perform local MSA and pair representation extraction while a central fusion server handles structure prediction. Outside research, I collaborate with Rikoukei Labs on marine AI and coral bleaching analysis.
